Part one: Fonts

I really wanted ot somethig wild for my display font, because I think it can alter the vibe of a webpage very well. On google fonts i found this interesting font called "Kumar". With those cut off edges it kind of feels like a Fraktur script, but it still looks modern.

The text font is Roboto. I tried Oswald at first until I heard that it's too condensed to be readable at that size. So I just picked Roboto as a safe choice
